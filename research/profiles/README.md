## Profile Structure

Each profile should include:

### Individual Profile Template
```markdown
# [Name]

## Basic Information
- **Full Name:** [Full legal name]
- **Known Aliases:** [Any aliases or pseudonyms]
- **Nationality:** [Country of origin/citizenship]
- **Profession:** [Primary occupation]
- **Date of Birth:** [if publicly available]
- **Location:** [General location, not exact address]

## Background
[Biographical information, education, career history]

## Public Presence
- Social media accounts
- Public statements
- Media appearances
- Published works

## Connection to Research
[Specific relevance to Information Terrorists research]

## Timeline of Activities
[Chronological list of relevant activities]

## Sources
[List of sources with citations]
```

### Organization Profile Template
```markdown
# [Organization Name]

## Basic Information
- **Full Name:** [Official organization name]
- **Type:** [Corporation/Non-profit/Association/etc.]
- **Founded:** [Year founded]
- **Headquarters:** [City, Country]
- **Industry:** [Primary industry/sector]

## Background
[History, mission, key activities]

## Key Personnel
[Leadership and notable members]

## Public Presence
- Official website
- Social media accounts
- Public statements
- Media coverage

## Connection to Research
[Specific relevance to Information Terrorists research]

## Timeline of Activities
[Chronological list of relevant activities]

## Sources
[List of sources with citations]
```

## Privacy and Legal Considerations

- Only include publicly available information
- Do not include private addresses, phone numbers, or personal contact information
- Respect privacy rights of individuals
- Document all sources
- Avoid speculation without evidence
