# Sources Directory

This directory contains source materials and references used in research.

## Subdirectories

- **news_articles/** - News articles from reputable publications
- **academic_papers/** - Peer-reviewed academic research papers
- **blog_posts/** - Expert blog posts and technical articles
- **social_media/** - Social media posts and content
- **official_documents/** - Government documents, reports, filings
- **archived_content/** - Archived versions of web content

## Source Quality Guidelines

**Tier 1 Sources (Highest Reliability):**
- Peer-reviewed academic papers
- Official government documents
- Reputable news organizations with editorial standards
- Court documents and legal filings

**Tier 2 Sources (Moderate Reliability):**
- Established news websites
- Expert blogs with credentials
- Industry reports
- Academic conference proceedings

**Tier 3 Sources (Use with Caution):**
- Social media posts
- Unverified online content
- Anonymous sources
- Opinion pieces without factual basis

## Source Documentation

Each source should include:
- Complete citation information
- Publication date
- Author/organization
- URL (if applicable)
- Archive URL (if available)
- Relevance to research

## Archiving

Always archive sources when possible using:
- Internet Archive (Wayback Machine)
- Archive.today
- Local PDF downloads
- Screenshot captures
